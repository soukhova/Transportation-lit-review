Standards

Population: Related to population/user characteristics (e.g., transport-related affordability level, perceived equitable trip, user experiences).

Opportunity: Access to destination indicator values (e.g., number of opportunities within 30-min transit trip, a ratio of opportunity per pop.) or the use of travel impedance (the cost/distance/time thresholds e.g., 300 m to bus stop).

Infrastructure: E.g., Level of service of bus infrastructure, quality of cycling infrastructure, universal design standards.

Environmental +: E.g., noise, air, pollution criteria, safety.
